Coffee Volume Required (ml):

Brewing Method:

  1. Boil a kettle of water.
  2. Rinse V60 paper into your mug to remove paper taste and pre-heat the mug.
  3. Grind 18g of coffee.
  4. Discard rinse water, pour coffee into V60 and make a little well with your finger.
  5. Start the timer and pour in 36g of water. Cover as much coffee as possible.
  6. Swirl the Mug/V60 with gusto until the brew almost goes over the lip.
  7. Wait 30-45s
  8. Take 30s to pour 180g of water in a spiral over the coffee.
  9. With a gentle pour, keep the brew topped up until you've poured 300g.
  10. With a teaspoon give the water a little stir in one direction and then the other. Trying to disturb the bed as little as possible and not leaving.
  11. Once the water has drained a little give it a gentle swirl.
  12. Wait for the coffee to drain, remove the V60 and enjoy!
